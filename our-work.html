Our Impact in Action

A visual journey through the community gardens, distribution drives, and workshops powered by your support.

Educational Workshops

Community Upliftment training

Providing structured, hands-on training and workshops focused on life skills and professional development to empower community members for self-sufficiency and improved quality of life.

Established Garden

Promoting Healthy a Lifestyle

Our mature garden areas offer a stable, high-yield food source. They are reliably cultivated to provide a consistent, diverse supply of fresh produce for our community distribution and nutrition programs.

Conference Events Participation

Spreading and Gaining Agricultural knowledge

Actively participating in national and international agricultural conferences to present research findings, network with industry experts, and gain advanced knowledge on sustainable farming techniques and crop management.

Community Gardens

New Site: Mophela Plot

Establishing and developing a new collaborative gardening space located in Mophela, designed to foster community engagement, promote sustainable food production, and offer educational opportunities.

Soup Kitchen

Food Sourcing

Operating a dedicated soup kitchen facility that provides hot, nutritious meals to support individuals and families facing food insecurity within the local area.

Broiler Farming

Meat Produce

Managing a controlled agricultural operation focused on the efficient and ethical raising of broiler chickens for high-volume, quality meat production and distribution.

Current Proposal: Mophela Backyard Gardens

"Mophela community faces challenges concerning food access and nutrition. Backyard gardening offers a solution by enabling residents to grow fresh produce which will reduce expenses and promote healthy lifestyle."

Key Objectives:

  • Improve access to fresh nutritious produce.
  • Support 100 households with establishing backyard gardens.
  • Increase community awareness about sustainable farming practises.
